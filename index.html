Iran Protest Map

This platform geolocates footage from recent protests in Iran on an interactive map. It features a timeline and charts which provide insights into the effectiveness of the internet shutdown as well as comparisons with previous periods of unrest. For transparency, verified and unverified footage is distinguished and varying estimates are given for deaths and arrests along with linked sources.

History Layered

Visualizing the entirety of Iranian history as a stratigraphic cross-section, with historical eras stacked as layers proportional to their duration. Drawing on the metaphor of sedimentary rock layers, this interactive experience illustrates the immense depth, longevity, and continuity of Iranian civilization.

Tech & Politics Timeline

This interactive chart explores the intersection of innovation and governance. By plotting major technological inventions alongside key milestones in political history, this application highlights the correlation between technological advancement and the evolution of increasingly complex political systems. The timeline highlights the need for new political systems to account for new leaps in technology.
