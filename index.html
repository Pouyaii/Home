Iran Protest Map

This platform geolocates footage from recent protests in Iran, clearly distinguishing between verified and unverified sources. The application features integrated charts and an interactive timeline, allowing users to trace the eruption of unrest and analyze protest activity levels specifically before and after the implementation of state-imposed internet blackouts.

Iran Layered

Iran Layered visualizes the entirety of Iranian history as a stratigraphic cross-section, with historical eras stacked as layers proportional to their duration. Drawing on the metaphor of sedimentary rock layers, this interactive experience illustrates the immense depth, longevity, and continuity of Iranian civilization.

Timelines

Timelines explores the intersection of innovation and governance. By plotting major technological inventions alongside key milestones in political history, this application highlights the correlation between technological advancement and the evolution of increasingly complex political systems.
